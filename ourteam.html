Our Team

The people making the magic happen

We're a passionate team of grocery veterans, technologists, and designers united by a single mission: making meal planning effortless for families everywhere.

Our Mission

To empower grocery chains to better serve their customers by providing innovative meal planning and shopping solutions—creating a future where customers feel supported, valued, and empowered to make smart decisions.

Guided by strong values

Our principles shape everything we do—from how we build our platform to how we support grocers and their customers.

Empower choice

We believe everyone deserves access to diverse, nutritious meals. Our tools help customers discover options that fit their lifestyle, budget, and dietary needs.

Drive efficiency

Time is precious. We build solutions that turn hours of meal planning into minutes, helping families spend less time stressing and more time together.

Ensure accessibility

Great food shouldn't be a luxury. We're committed to improving access to affordable, high-quality meals for every family, everywhere.

Reduce waste

We're on a mission to reduce food waste by helping shoppers buy what they need and use what they buy. Better planning means a healthier planet.

Build loyalty

We help grocers create meaningful connections with their customers through personalized experiences that keep them coming back.

Innovate simply

AI should feel effortless. We deliver powerful technology that's intuitive to use, making smart decisions feel natural for everyone.
