Our Story

Built from real life, for real families

Breez was born from one family's struggle—and a vision to make meal planning effortless for everyone.

Tal Zlotnitsky · Founder & CEO

"

Hey, I'm Tal.

When my son was young, meal planning used to be a nightmare for his mother and me. Raising a child with severe allergies to tree nuts and sesame turned what should have been simple family dinners into a stressful, repetitive chore.

We struggled to find meals that worked for everyone, and it felt like we were stuck in a cycle of the same boring dishes.

Years later, my daughter, a busy mom and nurse, shared her own challenges—trying to cook healthy, budget-friendly meals for her family of five while dealing with the guilt of wasted groceries.

It hit me: this wasn't just our problem; it was a problem for millions of families and individuals.

As someone who works with AI in tech, I realized there was a better way.

That's how Breez was born.

I wanted to create an app that could do more than just help with meal planning. I wanted it to make life easier, whether you're dealing with allergies, tight budgets, or just the daily grind. Breez is about turning meal planning from a chore into something that works for you, helping you enjoy diverse, nutritious meals with less stress and no waste.

I created Breez because I believe that with the right tools, we can all eat better, live healthier, and make our lives a little easier.

I hope Breez does that for you.

Tal

Tal Zlotnitsky

Founder & CEO, Breez AI
